## Search outcome

I did **not** find a second public dataset that explicitly documents **controlled traffic farming tramlines** and is clearly **L3-like** in the same way as your ROCK/DJI Rolling Hills benchmark. The strongest hits were three **vendor sample catalogues** that are genuinely agricultural and UAV-based, but each leaves some important metadata unstated. ## Confidence limitations

The main limitation is not lack of agricultural remote-sensing work; it is lack of **publicly released point-cloud datasets** behind that work. I found method papers on **wheel-track identification in wheat** and tramline-aware/agronomic UAV mapping, but the search surface did not expose matching open point-cloud downloads. Separately, several useful catalogues are **JS-heavy** or hide per-entry dataset links behind gallery pages or viewer redirects, which makes discovery harder than the content would suggest. Australian state repositories add another blind spot: repositories such as **Landgate** openly index LiDAR coverage, but they do not always expose a clean, one-click path from ÔÇťfarmland area of interestÔÇŁ to a ready-for-testing point-cloud download. ţłÇciteţłéturn15search1ţłéturn10view3ţłéturn9search12ţłéturn42view0ţłéturn30view0ţłéturn4view3ţłéturn11search5ţłü
